Plastic Pollution

All over the world, 400 million tons of plastic are produced annually, with about 8 million tons of plastic waste flowing into the sea.

Animals damage

Ocean animals suffer a lot of damage, such as mistaking marine waste for food or being caught in a net and unable to move.

Illegal Wastewater

Currently, it is right to send wastewater out after treatment, but there are still places where wastewater is illegally dumped.
